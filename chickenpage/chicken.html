Easy Roast Chicken with Pan Gravy




Ingredients:

1 whole chicken
Salt and pepper
1 chopped onion, 2 stalks chopped celery, 2large chopped carrots
1 sprig of thyme, 1 spring of rosemary, 1 bay leaf
1oz softened butter or vegetable oil
2 tblsp all-purpose flour
2 cups chicken stock

Recipe:

1.Season the cavity of the chicken with salt and pepper. Place the thyme, rosemary, and bayleaf inside the cavity.

2. Rub the skin of the chicken with butter or oil. Season the outside of the chicken with salt and pepper. Truss the chicken with string.

3. Heat an oven to 450 degrees Fahrenheit. Place the chicken, breast side up, on a rack in a roasting pan. Once he chicken has developed a golden brown appearance, turn down the temperature to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.

4. Roast the chicken for 45 minutes. Scatter the onion celery and carrot around the chicken and continue to roast until the thigh meat registers an internal temperature of 165 degrees Fahrenheit.

5. Remove the chicken from the oven and the roasting pan and allow it to rest for 10 minutes before serving.

6.Place the roasting pan on the stovetop and cook until the vegetables are browned and the fat is clear. Remove the vegetables and pour off all but 3 tbsp of the fat.

7. Add the flour and cook the roux for 2 minutes. Whisk in he stock until completely smooth.

8. Simmer the gravy until it reaches the desired consistency and flavor, 20 to 30 minutes. Remove the layer of fat on top of the gravy and adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper. Serve immediately with the chicken
